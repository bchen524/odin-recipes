Vegetarian Chili

A classic and hearty vegetarian chili

This vegetarian chili is a classic crowd-pleaser where your guests will never say "where's the beef?" Enjoy this delicately balanced and filling dish today.

Ingredients

  • Frozen burger crumbles
  • Black beans
  • Kidney beans
  • Tomato juice
  • Cumin
  • Bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients into a pot
  2. Bring to a simmer and cover
  3. Cook for 1-2 hours
  4. Garnish and let cool before serving
