Bread Sandwich

Don't make it too complicated: Bread Sandwich!

If your favorite part of a sandwich is the bread, give this recipe a try. Toasty outside with a soft filling, this sandwich is a good go-to for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Even the kids love it!

Ingredients

  • Sliced white bread

Directions

  1. Place a slice of bread on a plate or bowl
  2. Prepare another slice of bread separately
  3. Place another slice of bread on top of the first slice
  4. Place the prepared slice of bread on top of the other breads
