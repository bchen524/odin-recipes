Beany Beans

A classic, beans!

Scrumptious. Intuitive! Try this bean dish right now!

Ingredients

  • Black beans
  • Kidney beans
  • Garbanzo beans
  • Green beans
  • Salt

Directions

  1. Say to yourself "It's bean time!"
  2. Combine all beans in one pan
  3. Sear at high heat for 3-4 minutes
  4. Garnish with additional beans as necessary and serve.
